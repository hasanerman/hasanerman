### About Me

I am a software engineering student at OSTiM Technical University in Ankara, Turkey.
My areas of interest span a wide range of disciplines — from low-level embedded systems
and hardware-software integration to cross-platform desktop and mobile application development.
I am particularly drawn to fields where software meets the physical world, including
defense and aerospace systems.

---

### Focus Areas

**Embedded Systems**
Low-level programming, microcontroller development, real-time operating systems,
hardware-software interfacing and firmware design.

**Desktop Applications**
Building native and cross-platform desktop software with a focus on performance,
usability and system-level integration.

**Mobile Development**
Developing modern mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms, with interest
in performance-critical and real-time mobile software.

**Defense & Aerospace**
Interested in defense industry software, embedded avionics, command-control systems
and safety-critical software engineering.
